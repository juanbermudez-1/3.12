Gentle Haith

Intended for Begginers and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of
yoga this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana
(proper aligment and posture), prayama (breath work),
and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Althougth designed for intermidiate to advance students beginners are welcome to sample

Restorative Yoga

Thus 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by
a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice is can be a perfect way to help rehabiliate an injury.
